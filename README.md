## 🛡️ The "Shield of Humanity" (Cyber Security)
At $MAGER, cyber security is more than just protecting data—it's about protecting the plates of the hungry. 
* **Zero-Fatigue Operations:** Our team is split into shifts (Morning & Evening) to ensure 100% focus on quality and safety.
* **Cold-Storage Legacy:** 0.5% of our historical assets are stored in offline "Cold Devices" to ensure the shelter's operation remains immune to cyber threats.
* **Impact-Driven Coding:** Every transaction fee (Rp 500 - Rp 1,000) directly sustains this sanctuary.
